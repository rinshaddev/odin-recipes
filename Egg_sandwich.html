Egg sandwhich

sandwhich

Sand is the easy way to consume more calories and protein and it can be cooked under 15 min.

    Ingrediants

  • 2 slice raosted bread
  • 1 big egg
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 onion
  • 1 cabbage
  • Mayonise

    Way of making

  1. First fry the egg with carrot in it
  2. Take the bread .
  3. Place the fried egg in bread
  4. Put cabbage between the bread with mayonise

    5. I was willingly skipped some of the easy stages. The Egg sandwhich is ready

Oats meal recipe

oats meal in 10 min

This is super tasty oatsmeal . It so easy to to make
for those who have a little time could really helpful.

    Ingrediants

  • 1 cup of oats
    (lightly dry roasted for 4 min)
  • 3/4 cup of Water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoon coconut oil
  • 2 green chilli
  • 2 spring curry leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon Mustards
  • 1 onion cutted smally

    Start making

  1. Heat oil heavy bottem vessel.Once hot add mustard seeds and to splutter
  2. Add onion mix untill turn into lightly red
  3. Add curry leaves and green chilli mix it 4 min
  4. Add water and salt allow it boil
  5. Add oats to boiling water mix it for 2 min
